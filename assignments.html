Class Project
3 days ago

The assignment is out of 100 marks. It is 50% of the course weight.

In your groups of 3, you will plan, develop and test a user interface design. The project will consist of the following components:

  • Design Brief – 10 marks
  • User Research– 25 marks
  • Persona and a scenario
  • Proposed Task Flow and/or Site Map – 15 marks
  • Wire Frame – 5 marks
  • Mock Up – 5 marks
  • Prototype – 25 marks
* Please note that the deadlines are estimated and are subject to change. ** You will be receiving more detailed instructions on each component as we progress in the course.
